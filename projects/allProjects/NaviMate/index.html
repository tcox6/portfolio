NaviMate Browser Extension

NaviMate is a browser extension that I designed to restore users' last scroll position on webpages, facilitating smooth transitions between visits. Upon revisiting a webpage, NaviMate will automatically scroll to the user's last position on the page, and also stores their scroll positions on previous visits. Additionally, the extension allows users to manually add bookmarks (indicated by a red dot), and can be disabled on specific pages or websites.

This project was mostly an exploration of browser extensions (I learnt that they're a pain to work with). I originally intended to publish the project as an extension, but decided to withhold it due to persistent bugs. For example, the extension won't work on PDF documents, and will sometimes exhibit erroneous behaviour. Besides, it's actually not that useful...
